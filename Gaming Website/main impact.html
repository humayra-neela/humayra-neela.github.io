Community & Cultural Influence

PUBG introduced the battle royale genre to the masses, while Valorant revolutionized tactical shooters with hero mechanics.

Esports & Tournaments

Both games have established themselves in the global esports arena. Valorant has massive international tournaments like VCT, while PUBG hosts events such as PUBG Global Championship, attracting millions of viewers.

Online Communities

Online communities on Discord, Reddit, and YouTube are thriving. Creators, streamers, and fans come together to discuss strategies, share memes, and celebrate epic plays.

Lasting Legacy

These games didn’t just entertain — they shaped internet culture. Phrases like “Winner Winner Chicken Dinner” and “Clutch or Kick” are part of modern gaming slang.
